Basic Education Department

Providing quality education from Kindergarten through Senior High School, fostering academic growth, character development, and lifelong learning skills

Grade School

+

Our Grade School program focuses on providing a strong academic foundation for young learners. We emphasize the development of essential skills in reading, writing, mathematics, and science, while also nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and social skills. Our dedicated teachers create a supportive and engaging learning environment to help each student reach their full potential.

Junior High School

+

Our Junior High School program is designed to build on the foundational knowledge acquired in Grade School, while preparing students for the challenges of higher education. We offer a rigorous curriculum that includes advanced courses in mathematics, science, language arts, and social studies. Additionally, we provide opportunities for students to explore their interests through elective courses and extracurricular activities, fostering both academic and personal growth.

Senior High School

+

Our Senior High School program offers specialized tracks that prepare students for college and future careers. We provide a comprehensive education that includes core subjects, as well as electives in areas such as STEM, humanities, business, and the arts. Our goal is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and experiences necessary to succeed in their chosen fields and to become responsible, informed citizens.

Strands Offered:

  1. Humanities and Social Sciences
  2. Accountancy and Business Management
  3. Technical Vocational Track - Informations and Communications Technology
