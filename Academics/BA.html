Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration program provides students with a solid foundation in business principles and practices. It equips them with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in various business fields, including management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship. Our comprehensive curriculum combines theoretical learning with practical experience, preparing graduates to become effective leaders and innovators in the global business environment.
