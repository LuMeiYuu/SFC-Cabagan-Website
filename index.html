News And Events

December 13, 2024

SFC Cabagan Graduates Pass the Licensure Examination for Teachers

Saint Ferdinand College proudly celebrates the success of our September 2024 first-time takers. Our BEED program achieved an 80% passing rate, while our BSED program achieved a 62.50% passing rate. Congratulations to all our graduates for their hard work and dedication.
November 24, 2024

SFC-Cabagan Joins Christ the King Celebration

the students of Saint Ferdinand College-Cabagan took part in the solemn celebration of the Solemnity of Jesus Christ, the King of the Universe, also known as Christ the King Sunday. The occasion was marked by a Diocesan Concelebrated Mass held at St. Paul the Apostle Parish Church, where the faithful gathered to honor Christ as the King of the Universe.
October 16, 2024

SFC Peer Club Hosts Mental Health Awareness Booth

In line with Mental Health Awareness Month, the SFC Peer Club together with Madam April Ruth Neri, the school's counselor, set up a booth on October 16, 2024, promoting emotional well-being through fun activities, and open conversations. With the theme “It’s Time to Prioritize Our Mental Health.” The event aims to raise awareness among SFCians about the importance of mental health.
September 23, 2024

SFC Intramurals 2024 Opening Ceremony

The much-awaited SFC Cabagan Intramurals 2024 is officially underway! Students, faculty, and staff gathered to celebrate the opening ceremonies, kicking off a week of fun, sportsmanship, and healthy competition. From energetic cheers to the lighting of the torch, the excitement is in the air as we come together to cheer for our teams and show our school spirit.

Who We Are

Saint Ferdinand College is a private, Catholic coeducational basic and higher education institution in Ilagan City, Isabela, Philippines. It was established in 1950 by the Knights of Columbus Ilagan Council 3705.[3] Its formal operation began in school year 1951–1952, with classes offering from Kindergarten to College Level.
